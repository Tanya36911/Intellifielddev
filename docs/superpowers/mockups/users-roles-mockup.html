Admin 1
Owns the company. Configures hierarchy, surveys, payroll, users.
Sees: everything in the company
Manager 1
Oversees a branch. Assigns surveys, reviews compliance, approves payroll.
Sees: their node and everything below it
Rep 2
Field user. Completes assigned surveys at their stores.
Sees: stores in their node only
A role is what a person can do. Their pin is where they can do it. Pin someone to a node and they automatically see that node and everything beneath it, never a sibling branch and never another company. This is the same security rule the backend enforces on every request.

Intelli has three fixed roles. A role decides what someone can do; their pin decides where. An admin pins at the company root and sees everything. A manager pins at any branch and is scoped to it (including reps and managers pinned below them). A rep pins at the level above Store. This reference is read-only.
